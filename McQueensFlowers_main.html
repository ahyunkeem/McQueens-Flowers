MCQUEENS FLOWER SCHOOL

More View

Trailblazers in the world of floristry education since 1999 - McQueens Flower School London are in the unique position where they teach what they do and they do what they teach. Whether you’re dreaming of having a career change or would like to escape the hustle and bustle of the outside world to one of our creative workshops - you’ll be guided by our team of expert tutors every step of the way.

McQueens Flowers School is ideally located in the heart of London's Mayfair - just minutes from Bond Street Station. The beautifully renovated building comprising of two expertly appointed classroom-studio spaces, has been furnished to provide an exceptional learning environment.
